The IPL Auction for the 2021 season is to be held on 18th February at Chennai. There are 61 remaining slots for players to be picked, and over 1100 players have registered for the event. The hype leading to the D-day has been massive and there is a huge expectation for the foreign picks to go grab the big bucks. Among the foreigners, the maximum entries have come from the West Indies.
There have been a few surprises though, with players like Mitchell Starc and James Pattinson opting out for this year. Starc was bound to possibly get the highest bid, with many teams in search of a frontline pacer.Pattinson's decision too came as a surprise for the experts, given his good run in the last edition at the UAE.
